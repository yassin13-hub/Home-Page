Personnal Information

Yassine BENDIMERAD/ PhD-MSc-BSc

Assistant Professor, University of Bechar, Algeria

Digital Communication (ComNum) Team, LTT Laboratory of Telecommunications

Dr.M.Y.BENDIMERAD received the B.Sc. degree in electrical and electronic engineering and the M.Sc.,Eng. and Ph.D degrees in telecommunication and wireless communication technologies from the University of Tlemcen, Algeria, and INP Grenoble "Polytechnic Institute of Technology", France in 2010, 2012, 2015 and 2016, respectively. He is an Assistant Professor with the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, University of Bechar since 2017. He is a Researcher member at the Digital Communication (ComNum) Team, LTT Laboratoty of Telecommunication, University of Tlemcen. His research interests include Wireless communication and mobile networks, massive MIMO/MIMO systems, energy efficiency in Wireless systems and RF impairments mitigation. He has authored several papers in international conferences and journals including IET and IEEE. He has participated in several national and international research projects as PHC-TASSILI Project and also collaborates as a Reviewer for international journals included in the JCR.
